Who Should attend:

In-house counsels, as well as lawyers (legal counsels), arbitrators, and representatives of national and international chambers (banking, insurance, etc.) as well as all professionals interested in and/or involved in arbitration

Early Bird Registration Fee (until 6 January 2025) – 6,500 CZK / 250 EUR incl. VAT per person
Regular Registration Fee (from 7 January 2025) – 7,500 CZK / 290 EUR incl. VAT per person
